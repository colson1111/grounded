# Privacy Policy — Grounded

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Overview

Grounded is a screen time and focus app for iOS. We take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Grounded collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

## Data Collection

Grounded does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, no backend server, and no analytics.

All data created by Grounded — including your profiles, schedules, blocked app selections, anchor objects, and usage history — is stored locally on your device in Apple's secure app container. It never leaves your device.

## Camera

Grounded uses your device camera to scan anchor objects (using on-device object recognition) and QR codes. Camera data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never stored or transmitted.

## Screen Time API

Grounded uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls) to apply app and website restrictions. This integration is handled entirely by iOS. Grounded does not have access to your Screen Time data or usage history beyond what you explicitly configure within the app.

## Third-Party Services

Grounded uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

## Children

Grounded is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect it.
