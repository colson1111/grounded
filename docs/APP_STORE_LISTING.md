## Short Description (170 chars)
Put the phone down. Grounded blocks distracting apps and sites on a schedule or on demand, and asks for a small moment of intention before you return.

## Long Description
Your phone can wait.

Grounded helps you step away from the apps and sites that pull you out of the moment — whether that's focused work, family time, or sleep. Not a productivity tool. A pause for your mind.

Set up a profile for each part of your day. Work keeps social media out of reach. Family keeps everything quiet. Sleep means sleep. Each profile blocks exactly what you choose, using Apple's Screen Time — so it holds even when Grounded isn't open.

No gadget required. Other apps make you buy a physical device and place it somewhere in your home. Grounded lets you pick any real-world object as your anchor — something that already means you're done. Your Work profile unlocks at the coffee maker when the workday ends. Your Sleep profile unlocks when you're actually out of bed in the morning. Your Family profile unlocks at your car keys on the way out the door. The friction lives where it makes sense for your life, not wherever a $30 device happened to land.

Schedules ease you in too. Set a time window and Grounded activates quietly in the background. Coming back is always a small moment of intention — not a punishment, just a pause.

Your data stays on your device. No account. No backend. No tracking.

One-time purchase. No subscription.
